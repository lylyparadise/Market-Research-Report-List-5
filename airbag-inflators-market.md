<p><p>Airbag inflators are safety devices in vehicles that deploy airbags in the event of a collision. There are three main types of airbag inflators: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. </p><p>Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly inflate the airbag. Stored gas inflators store compressed gas that is released upon impact to inflate the airbag. Hybrid inflators combine the features of pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators for an efficient and reliable inflation process.</p></p>
