<p><strong>The Porous Metal Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent Hole Type</li><li>Continuous Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The porous metal material market is typically categorized into two main types: independent hole type and continuous hole type. The independent hole type features discrete, non-connected voids within the metal structure, allowing for selective fluid or gas flow while maintaining strength. In contrast, the continuous hole type has interconnected pores, enabling free passage of fluids and gases throughout the material. Each type serves specific applications, such as filtration, catalysis, and lightweight structural components, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
