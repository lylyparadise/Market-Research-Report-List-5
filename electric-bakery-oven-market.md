<p><p>The electric bakery oven market is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer ovens. Single-layer ovens are designed for smaller bakeries or operations requiring limited production capacity, offering straightforward functionality and ease of use. In contrast, multi-layer ovens are suited for larger-scale operations, allowing multiple trays to bake simultaneously, thereby increasing efficiency and output. Both types cater to different baking needs, with single-layer ovens often preferred for artisanal baking, while multi-layer ovens are ideal for mass production and diverse product ranges.</p></p>
