<p><p>The space-based smart sensors market is divided into two primary types: radiation-hardened and radiation-tolerant sensors. Radiation-hardened sensors are designed to withstand high levels of ionizing radiation, ensuring reliable performance in harsh environments, such as deep space missions. In contrast, radiation-tolerant sensors can operate effectively in moderate radiation levels but do not offer the same level of robustness as hardened sensors. Both types are crucial for satellite operations, enabling enhanced data collection and monitoring in various space applications.</p></p>
