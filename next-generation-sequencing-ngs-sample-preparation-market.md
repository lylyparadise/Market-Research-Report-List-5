<p><strong>The Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Sample Preparation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS)</li><li>Ion Semiconductor Sequencing</li><li>Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) Sequencing</li><li>Nanopore Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) sample preparation encompasses various technologies tailored for high-throughput genomic analysis. Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) relies on fluorescently labeled nucleotides, enabling accurate base detection. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing measures ion releases during DNA polymerization, offering rapid results. Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) Sequencing involves real-time observation of DNA synthesis, enhancing accuracy and length. Nanopore Sequencing utilizes nanopores to detect individual nucleotides as they pass through, providing ultra-long reads and portability for diverse applications in genomics.</p></p>
