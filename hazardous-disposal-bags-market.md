<p><p>The hazardous disposal bags market comprises three primary types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE bags are strong and resistant to punctures, making them ideal for sharp waste. LDPE bags offer flexibility and are less rigid, suited for general hazardous waste. LLDPE bags combine features of both, providing enhanced strength and stretchability, making them effective for various hazardous materials. These bag types cater to diverse needs in medical, industrial, and laboratory settings.</p></p>
