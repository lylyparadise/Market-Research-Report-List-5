<p><p>The magnetic field sensing ICs market encompasses various technologies that detect magnetic fields for applications in automotive, industrial, consumer electronics, and more. Hall Effect Sensing ICs utilize the Hall effect to measure magnetic fields, widely used in position sensing. TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) Sensing ICs offer high sensitivity and low power consumption, ideal for precision applications. AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) Sensing ICs provide robust performance in varying conditions, while GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) Sensing ICs deliver enhanced sensitivity and are commonly employed in data storage and automotive systems.</p></p>
