<p><ul><li>Air Cycle Air Conditioning</li><li>Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Cooling Systems market primarily includes two types: Air Cycle Air Conditioning and Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning. </p><p>Air Cycle Air Conditioning utilizes ambient air, compressing and cooling it to regulate cabin temperature, promoting efficiency and lightweight designs. In contrast, Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning relies on refrigerants that change states to absorb and dissipate heat, ensuring effective climate control in various environmental conditions. Both systems are critical for maintaining passenger comfort and operational performance in modern aircraft.</p></p>
