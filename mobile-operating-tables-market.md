<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile operating tables market is segmented into four main types: manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Manual tables require physical effort to adjust positions, making them cost-effective but labor-intensive. Electric tables offer ease of use with powered adjustments for height and tilt, enhancing efficiency. Hydraulic tables use fluid power for adjustments, providing smooth movement and stability. Electro-hydraulic tables combine electric and hydraulic systems, offering precise control and versatility, making them increasingly popular in modern surgical settings.</p></p>
