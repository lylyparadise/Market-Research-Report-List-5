<p><ul><li>Periapical Abscess</li><li>Periodontal Abscess</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental infection treatment market is categorized into three main types: periapical abscess, periodontal abscess, and others. Periapical abscess refers to infections occurring at the tooth's root, often resulting from untreated cavities or trauma, requiring treatments like root canal therapy. Periodontal abscess involves infections in the gums, often linked to periodontal disease, necessitating procedures like scaling or surgery. The "others" category encompasses various oral infections, including osteomyelitis or dental cysts, requiring diverse treatment approaches depending on severity and location.</p></p>
