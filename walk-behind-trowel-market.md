<p><ul><li>Hydrostatic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walk-behind trowel market is primarily divided into two types: hydrostatic and mechanical. Hydrostatic trowels use hydraulic systems for smooth operation, offering better control and efficiency, particularly on larger surfaces. They are favored for their ease of use and reduced operator fatigue. In contrast, mechanical trowels utilize a traditional gear system, making them more robust and often more affordable. They are ideal for smaller projects but require more physical effort, making them suitable for specific applications where budget constraints are a priority.</p></p>
