<p><strong>The Industrial Rectifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCR</li><li>MCR</li><li>HCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Rectifiers market is categorized into three main types: Low Current Rectifiers (LCR), Medium Current Rectifiers (MCR), and High Current Rectifiers (HCR). LCRs are designed for low-power applications, typically under 50 A, making them suitable for smaller industrial processes. MCRs handle medium power levels, usually between 50 A and 2000 A, used in various manufacturing setups. HCRs cater to high-power demands, exceeding 2000 A, essential for large-scale industrial operations like electrolysis and metal processing.</p></p>
