<p><p>The screen printing ink market is categorized into Type I and Type II segments based on the ink formulation and application. In contrast, Type II inks are water-based, offering a more eco-friendly option with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). These inks are often preferred for textile printing and are gaining popularity due to increasing environmental regulations and consumer demand for sustainable products.</p></p>
