<p><p>Titanium heat exchangers are utilized in various industries for efficient heat transfer. The two main types of titanium heat exchangers are shell and tube heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of tubes within a cylindrical shell, allowing for high heat transfer efficiency. Plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, use metal plates to facilitate heat exchange. Both types of heat exchangers offer advantages in terms of durability, corrosion resistance, and thermal conductivity, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
