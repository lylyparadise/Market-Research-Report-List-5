The Spiral Groove Type features a helical design that enhances torque transmission while accommodating misalignment, making it ideal for dynamic applications. Conversely, the Parallel Groove Type employs grooves oriented parallel to the coupling axis, providing a robust connection with high flexibility and durability under varying loads. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive and machinery, where efficient power transmission and vibration dampening are essential.</p></p>
