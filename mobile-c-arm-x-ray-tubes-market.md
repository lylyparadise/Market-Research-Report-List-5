<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile C-arm X-ray tubes market is categorized into two main types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes are simpler in design and offer good durability and reliability, making them suitable for basic imaging needs. In contrast, rotating anode tubes provide higher heat management and allow for quicker exposure times, enhancing image quality during dynamic procedures. The choice between these types depends on the specific imaging requirements, operational efficiency, and budget constraints of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
