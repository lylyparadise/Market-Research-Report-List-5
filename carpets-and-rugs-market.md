<p><p>The carpets and rugs market encompasses various types, each characterized by distinct manufacturing techniques. Woven carpets utilize looms to interlace fibers, ensuring durability and intricate designs. Needle felt carpets are produced by bonding fibers with barbed needles, resulting in a dense, durable fabric ideal for high-traffic areas. Knotted carpets, often handmade, feature intricate patterns created by knotting yarn around the foundation, showcasing craftsmanship. Other types include tufted and bonded options, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
