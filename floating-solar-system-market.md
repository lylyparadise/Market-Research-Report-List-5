PV modules are the solar panels that convert sunlight into electricity, while floating bodies and anchoring systems provide stability and support for the panels on water bodies. Inverters are essential for converting the DC electricity generated by the panels into AC electricity for use in the grid. Other components may include monitoring systems, cables, and control units to ensure efficient operation of the floating solar system.</p></p>
