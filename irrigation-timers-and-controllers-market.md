<p><p>Irrigation timers and controllers come in three main types: smart controllers, tap timers, and basic controllers. Smart controllers are advanced systems that adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions and soil moisture levels. Tap timers are simple devices that attach to outdoor faucets and allow for manual control of watering. Basic controllers are more traditional systems that allow users to set specific watering times and durations. Each type of controller offers different features and levels of automation to suit varying irrigation needs.</p></p>
