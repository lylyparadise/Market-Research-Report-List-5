<p><p>Metal forming machine tools market is segmented into bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling to shape metal into a desired form. Sheet forming includes processes such as bending, deep drawing, and stamping to create complex shapes from sheet metal. Powder metal forming utilizes powdered metal particles that are compacted and sintered to produce intricate and precise components. These different market types cater to various industries and applications in the metal forming sector.</p></p>
