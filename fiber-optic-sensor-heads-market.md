<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensor Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic</li><li>Extrinsic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensor heads can be categorized into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic sensors utilize the optical fiber itself as the sensing element, where changes in the light properties within the fiber indicate environmental changes. In contrast, extrinsic sensors employ external components to detect changes, with the fiber acting as a transmission medium for the signal to a detection system. Both types are used in various applications, including temperature, pressure, and strain measurement, offering high sensitivity and immune to electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
