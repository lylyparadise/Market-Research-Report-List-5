<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Concrete (GFRC) is a composite material that incorporates glass fibers into concrete, enhancing its strength and durability. The GFRC market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Sprayed GFRC** involves applying a mixture of concrete and glass fibers using spray techniques, ideal for intricate shapes. </p><p>2. **Premix GFRC** involves blending glass fibers directly into the concrete before casting, suitable for various applications. </p><p>3. **Hybrid GFRC** combines both techniques, leveraging benefits from each to optimize performance and design versatility.</p></p>
