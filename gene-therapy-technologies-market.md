<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene therapy technologies market can be categorized into Type I and Type II segments. Type I focuses on in vivo gene therapy, where therapeutic genes are directly delivered into patients' cells, targeting diseases at the cellular level. In contrast, Type II encompasses ex vivo gene therapy, where cells are modified outside the body before being transplanted back into the patient. Both approaches aim to address genetic disorders and enhance treatment effectiveness, offering innovative solutions in the field of personalized medicine.</p></p>
