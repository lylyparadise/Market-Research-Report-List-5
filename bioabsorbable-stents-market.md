<p><p>The bioabsorbable stents market is segmented into two primary types: peripheral bioabsorbable stents and coronary bioabsorbable stents. Peripheral bioabsorbable stents are designed for treating vascular blockages in peripheral arteries, offering benefits such as reduced inflammation and improved long-term outcomes. Coronary bioabsorbable stents, on the other hand, are utilized in coronary arteries to restore blood flow while gradually dissolving over time, minimizing complications associated with permanent stents and promoting natural vessel healing. Both types enhance patient safety and recovery.</p></p>
