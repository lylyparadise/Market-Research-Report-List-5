<p><p>The atherectomy devices market includes several types, each suited for specific vascular procedures. Directional atherectomy utilizes a rotating blade to remove plaque from the arterial walls. Rotational atherectomy employs a high-speed rotating burr to grind away obstructive tissue. Orbital atherectomy uses an oscillating elliptical cutting mechanism to efficiently clear blockages.
