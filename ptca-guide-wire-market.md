<p><p>The PTCA guide wire market comprises two main types: solid steel wires and nitinol core wires. Solid steel wires are characterized by their rigidity and strength, providing excellent pushability and feedback during procedures. Conversely, nitinol core wires are made from a flexible, shape-memory alloy, offering enhanced maneuverability and the ability to navigate complex vascular pathways. Both types serve critical roles in percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty, aiding in the delivery of balloons and stents for cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
