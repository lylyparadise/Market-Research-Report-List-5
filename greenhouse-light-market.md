<p><p>The greenhouse light market consists of three primary types: LED, sodium lamps, and halogen lamps. LED lights are energy-efficient, providing a broad spectrum that promotes plant growth while consuming less energy. Sodium lamps, particularly high-pressure sodium (HPS), are favored for their intensity and effectiveness in flowering stages but can generate significant heat. Halogen lamps offer higher brightness and improved efficiency over traditional incandescent lights, but they consume more energy and produce more heat than LEDs. Each type caters to specific greenhouse needs.</p></p>
