<p><p>The 3D dental scanner market comprises two primary types: 3D dental light scanners and 3D dental laser scanners. 3D dental light scanners use structured light to capture detailed images of dental structures quickly and accurately, making them ideal for creating digital impressions. In contrast, 3D dental laser scanners utilize laser technology to scan and capture precise measurements, providing high-definition images for diagnosis and treatment planning. Both technologies are essential for enhancing workflow efficiency and improving patient outcomes in modern dental practices.</p></p>
