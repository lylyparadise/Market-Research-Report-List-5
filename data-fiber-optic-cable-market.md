<p><strong>The Data Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data Fiber Optic Cable Market is segmented into two main types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode cables are designed for long-distance communication and have a smaller core size, allowing only one mode of light to pass through. Multimode cables have a larger core size, enabling multiple modes of light to pass through, making them suitable for short-distance communications. Single Mode cables are commonly used in telecommunication networks and long-distance transmissions, while Multimode cables are more often used in data centers and LAN environments.</p></p>
