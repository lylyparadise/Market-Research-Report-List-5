<p><p>The Automated Retractable Needle Safety Syringe market consists of two main types: vacuum operated and spring operated syringes. Vacuum operated syringes utilize a vacuum mechanism to retract the needle after use, ensuring safety and preventing needle-stick injuries. In contrast, spring operated syringes use a spring mechanism for needle retraction, providing rapid and reliable closure post-injection. Both types enhance user safety, reduce the risk of infection, and promote proper disposal practices, driving their adoption in healthcare settings.</p></p>
