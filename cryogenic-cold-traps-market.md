<p><p>The cryogenic cold traps market comprises two main types: embedded cold traps and split cold traps. Embedded cold traps are integrated within systems, providing efficient cooling and vapor capturing in compact spaces, making them ideal for laboratories and industrial applications. In contrast, split cold traps feature a modular design, allowing flexibility in placement and maintenance. This separation enhances temperature control and simplifies cleaning processes. Both types serve critical roles in preserving samples and optimizing performance in cryogenic environments.</p></p>
