<p><p>The hemorrhoid treatment instrument market comprises various devices tailored for effective management of hemorrhoids. Band ligators are used for rubber band ligation, which cuts off blood supply to hemorrhoids. Sclerotherapy injectors deliver a sclerosing agent, shrinking hemorrhoids. Infrared coagulators use heat to close off veins, while bipolar probes provide precise coagulation through electrical current. Cryotherapy devices employ extreme cold to reduce hemorrhoid size and alleviate symptoms. Together, these instruments enhance treatment options for patients suffering from hemorrhoids.</p></p>
