<p><ul><li>Prime Lens</li><li>Zoom Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photographic lens market is primarily divided into two categories: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses feature a fixed focal length, offering superior image quality, wider apertures, and lighter designs, making them ideal for specialized photography like portraiture and low-light conditions. In contrast, zoom lenses provide a versatile range of focal lengths, allowing for greater flexibility in framing shots without changing lenses. This adaptability makes zoom lenses popular among casual photographers and those seeking convenience in diverse shooting environments.</p></p>
