<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Platelet-rich Plasma Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure PRP</li><li>Leucocyte-rich PRP</li><li>Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet-rich Plasma (PRP) therapy includes several types that cater to various medical and aesthetic needs. Pure PRP is derived primarily from the patient’s blood and contains high concentrations of platelets with minimal leukocytes, promoting healing. Leucocyte-rich PRP contains higher levels of white blood cells, enhancing its immune response and potential healing properties. Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF) combines platelets and leukocytes in a fibrin matrix, offering sustained release of growth factors for tissue regeneration, making it suitable for surgical and cosmetic applications.</p></p>
