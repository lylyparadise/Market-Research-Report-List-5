<p><p>The sugar-derived surfactant market features key types including alkyl polyglycosides (APGs), decyl glucoside, and sucrose cocoate. Alkyl polyglycosides are non-ionic surfactants known for their mildness and biodegradability, commonly used in personal care products. Decyl glucoside is another gentle surfactant, derived from corn glucose and coconut oil, widely utilized in formulations for sensitive skin. Sucrose cocoate, derived from sugar and coconut oil, is valued for its emulsifying and thickening properties, enhancing product stability and performance.</p></p>
