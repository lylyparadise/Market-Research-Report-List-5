<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) Resin market is segmented into two main types - Emulsion Graft Copolymerization and Bulk Copolymerization. Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves the addition of grafting monomers into a preformed emulsion, resulting in enhanced properties such as impact strength and heat resistance. On the other hand, Bulk Copolymerization involves the direct polymerization of monomers in a bulk phase, leading to a more cost-effective and efficient process for producing ABS Resin. These two methods cater to different market needs and applications within the ABS Resin industry.</p></p>
