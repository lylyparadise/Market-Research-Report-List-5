<p><p>The Graphitized Cathode Block market includes three main types: Semi-Graphitic, Graphitic, and Graphitized. Semi-Graphitic blocks contain a mix of graphite and carbon, offering moderate conductivity and thermal resistance. Graphitic blocks have higher graphite content, providing improved conductivity and durability. Graphitized blocks are fully graphitized, delivering superior electrical and thermal performance, making them ideal for high-demand applications like aluminum smelting. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing cost, performance, and longevity in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
