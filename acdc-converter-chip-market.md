<p><ul><li>Linear AC DC converters</li><li>SMPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC/DC converter chip market comprises two main types: linear AC/DC converters and switch-mode power supplies (SMPS). Linear converters provide a simple, low-noise output by regulating voltage through resistive dissipation, making them ideal for low-power applications. In contrast, SMPS utilize high-frequency switching to convert AC to DC, offering higher efficiency and compact designs for a broader range of applications. The choice between these types depends on factors such as power requirements, efficiency, and application-specific needs.</p></p>
