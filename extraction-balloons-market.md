<p><p>The extraction balloons market is comprised of two main types: triple-lumen and double-lumen balloons. Triple-lumen balloons feature three channels, allowing for simultaneous aspiration, injection, and drainage, enhancing efficiency during endoscopic procedures. Double-lumen balloons, on the other hand, have two channels, typically for aspiration and delivery, facilitating effective tissue retrieval and fluid management. Both types are essential in medical procedures, particularly in gastroenterology, where they optimize the extraction process and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
