<p><p>The carbon fiber composite market is dominated by three main types: pitch-based carbon fiber, polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based carbon fibers, and rayon-based carbon fiber. Pitch-based carbon fibers offer high thermal and electrical conductivity, making them suitable for specialized applications. PAN-based carbon fibers are widely used due to their excellent mechanical properties and lightweight nature, often used in aerospace and automotive industries. Rayon-based carbon fibers, while less common, provide cost-effective solutions for various applications, focusing on moderate performance and affordability.</p></p>
