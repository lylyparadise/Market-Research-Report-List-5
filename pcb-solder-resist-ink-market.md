<p><p>The PCB solder resist ink market comprises three main types: photoimageable SRI, UV curable SRI, and thermal curable SRI. Photoimageable SRI is primarily used to create precise patterns through exposure to light, allowing for high-resolution designs. UV curable SRI hardens when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering quick processing times and durability. Thermal curable SRI requires heat to cure, providing excellent adhesion and resistance. Each type serves specific applications in ensuring effective insulation and protection for circuit boards.</p></p>
