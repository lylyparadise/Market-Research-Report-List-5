<p><strong>The Cryonics Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slow freezing</li><li>Vitrification</li><li>Ultra-rapid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryonics technology encompasses three main market types: slow freezing, vitrification, and ultra-rapid freezing. Slow freezing involves gradually lowering temperature to minimize ice crystal formation, but risks damage to cells. Vitrification employs a glass-like state by replacing water with cryoprotectants, preventing crystal formation and offering better preservation. Ultra-rapid freezing freezes tissues almost instantly, reducing ice damage but posing challenges in maintaining cellular integrity. Each method aims to preserve biological structures for potential revival in the future, with varying degrees of effectiveness and risk.</p></p>
