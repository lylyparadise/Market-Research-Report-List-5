Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and offer easy application with rapid curing times. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, typically provide superior adhesion and chemical resistance, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Each type serves specific needs depending on the application requirements and desired performance.</p></p>
