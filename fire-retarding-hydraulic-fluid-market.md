<p><p>The fire-retarding hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **HFA**: Water-containing fluids that primarily use water as a base, providing good fire resistance and lubrication. </p><p>2. **HFB**: Water-containing fluids with added oils, offering enhanced lubrication and fire safety. </p><p>3. **HFC**: Water-containing fluids that include synthetic additives for improved thermal stability and fire resistance. </p><p>4. **HFD**: Non-water-containing fluids, typically synthetic, designed for high-temperature environments with excellent fire resistance properties. Each type serves specific industrial applications where fire hazards are a concern.</p></p>
