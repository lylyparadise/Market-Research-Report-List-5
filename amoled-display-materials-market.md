<p><strong>The AMOLED Display Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED display materials market includes two primary types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) displays and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED technology utilizes an active matrix of thin-film transistors for pixel control, allowing for higher resolution, faster refresh rates, and superior color accuracy, making it ideal for smartphones and high-definition displays. In contrast, PMOLED relies on a simpler passive matrix for control, leading to lower resolution and limited applications mainly in small and low-power displays, such as wearables and basic screens.</p></p>
