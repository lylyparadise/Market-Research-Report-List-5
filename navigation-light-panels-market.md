<p><p>The navigation light panels market can be categorized into three main types: AC Navigation Light Panels, DC Navigation Light Panels, and Others. AC Navigation Light Panels utilize alternating current, typically found in larger vessels, offering stable and reliable power for navigation lights. DC Navigation Light Panels, powered by direct current, are common in smaller boats and offer efficiency and portability. The "Others" category encompasses specialized or hybrid systems that may blend features of both AC and DC panels, catering to unique maritime needs.</p></p>
