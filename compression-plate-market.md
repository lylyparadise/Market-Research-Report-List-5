<p><p>Compression plates are orthopedic devices utilized in the fixation of fractured bones. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period. </p><p>Latest trends indicate a shift towards minimally invasive surgeries which require specialized compression plates tailored for such procedures.
