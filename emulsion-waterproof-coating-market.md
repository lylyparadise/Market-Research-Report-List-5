<p><p>The emulsion waterproof coating market comprises two primary types: dry and liquid. Dry coatings typically come in powder form and are mixed with water before application, offering easy storage and transport. They provide strong adhesion and durability once applied. In contrast, liquid coatings are pre-mixed and ready for immediate use, enabling quick application and even coverage. Both types are designed to prevent water penetration, protect surfaces from moisture damage, and enhance the longevity of structures.</p></p>
