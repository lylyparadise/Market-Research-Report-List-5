<p><p>The reverse shoulder arthroplasty market consists of various types, primarily focusing on total shoulder arthroplasty and ream and run procedures. Total shoulder arthroplasty involves replacing the shoulder joint with prosthetic components to restore functionality, ideal for conditions like arthritis. Conversely, the ream and run approach emphasizes preserving the native bone while reshaping the glenoid socket to optimize biomechanics, catering to younger, active patients. Both methods aim to alleviate pain and improve mobility, each tailored to specific patient needs.</p></p>
