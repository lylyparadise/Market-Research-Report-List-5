<p><p>Fuel Management Systems (FMS) can be categorized into two main types: card-based and on-site markets. Card-based systems utilize fuel cards to track and manage fuel purchases at various stations, enabling centralized monitoring of expenditures and usage. In contrast, on-site markets involve managing fuel supply directly at a location, such as depots or fleets, with integrated systems for real-time tracking and inventory control. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce fraud, and optimize fuel management for businesses.</p></p>
