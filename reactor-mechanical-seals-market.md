<p><p>The reactor mechanical seals market comprises three main types: single end seals, double-sided seals, and multi-faceted seals. Single end seals feature a seal face that is located at one end, primarily used in simpler applications with lowpressure requirements. Double-sided seals create a barrier on both sides, enhancing reliability and preventing leaks in more demanding conditions. Multi-faceted seals offer multiple sealing surfaces to accommodate various process requirements, improving efficiency and durability in complex systems, making them suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
