<p><p>Potassium Sulphate (SOP) is a vital potassium fertilizer, essential for promoting plant health and enhancing crop yield. The market for SOP is primarily divided into two types: Powder SOP and Granular SOP. Powder SOP is finely ground, facilitating quick absorption by plants, making it suitable for various high-value applications. In contrast, Granular SOP offers slower release characteristics, allowing for efficient nutrient delivery over time, which is ideal for large-scale agricultural use and soil conditioning. Both forms cater to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
