<p><p>The off-line LED drivers market is categorized into DC power and AC power segments. DC power drivers are designed to convert the direct current voltage from a source, ensuring efficient energy management for LED applications. In contrast, AC power drivers convert alternating current from mains electricity, providing an easy connection for lighting fixtures without additional power conversion. Both types cater to varying application needs, with DC solutions typically favoring compact designs and efficiency, while AC solutions offer broader compatibility with standard electrical systems.</p></p>
