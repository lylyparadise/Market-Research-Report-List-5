<p><p>The Active Noise and Vibration Control Systems market comprises two primary types: Active Noise Control (ANC) Systems and Active Vibration Control (AVC) Systems. ANC Systems utilize microphones and speakers to detect and cancel ambient noise through sound wave interference, enhancing audio quality and comfort in various environments. Conversely, AVC Systems employ sensors and actuators to detect and mitigate vibrations in structures and machinery, improving safety and performance. Both systems are essential in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction for enhanced operational efficiency.</p></p>
