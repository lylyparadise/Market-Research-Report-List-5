<p><p>The surgical adhesion barrier market comprises three main formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations are thin layers that provide a barrier against tissue sticking and are often used in laparoscopic surgeries. Gel formulations are viscous substances that can conform to irregular surfaces, offering flexibility and coverage during procedures. Liquid formulations are fluid solutions that easily distribute over tissue, creating a protective barrier. Each type is designed to minimize postoperative adhesions, enhancing patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
