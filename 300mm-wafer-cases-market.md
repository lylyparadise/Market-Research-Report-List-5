<p><p>The 300mm Wafer Cases market primarily consists of two types: Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). FOSBs are designed for the transportation and storage of wafers, offering protection and easy access for handling. In contrast, FOUPs are specialized containers that ensure wafer integrity during transport within semiconductor fabrication facilities. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining wafer quality and improving efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing processes while accommodating the increasing demand for advanced technologies.</p></p>
