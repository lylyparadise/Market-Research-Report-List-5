<p><strong>The UV Cured Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cured printing inks utilize ultraviolet light to quickly harden or cure the ink during the printing process. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Arc Curing and LED Curing. Arc curing employs traditional mercury vapor lamps to emit UV light, offering high intensity and wide spectrum capabilities, but at higher energy costs. In contrast, LED curing uses light-emitting diodes for curing, providing energy efficiency, extended lamp life, and lower heat output, making it suitable for heat-sensitive substrates.</p></p>
