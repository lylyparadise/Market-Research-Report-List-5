<p><p>Ultrasonic motion sensors are devices that utilize ultrasonic waves to detect the presence and movement of objects within a specified range. <p><strong>The Ultrasonic Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proximity Detection</li><li>Ranging Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic motion sensor market includes two primary types: proximity detection and ranging measurement. Proximity detection uses ultrasonic waves to identify the presence and movement of objects within a specified area, commonly used in security systems and automated lighting. Ranging measurement, on the other hand, calculates the distance between the sensor and an object by measuring the time it takes for emitted ultrasonic waves to return. This technology is utilized in applications such as robotics, industrial automation, and automotive systems.</p></p>
