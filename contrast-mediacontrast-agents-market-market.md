<p><p>The contrast media/agents market comprises several types that enhance imaging in medical diagnostics. Iodinated contrast agents are commonly used for X-rays and CT scans, improving visualization of blood vessels and organs. Gadolinium-based agents are primarily used in MRI, enhancing soft tissue contrast. Barium contrast is employed in gastrointestinal imaging, aiding in assessing the digestive tract. Microbubbles, used in ultrasound, enhance echogenicity for clearer vascular imaging. Each type plays a vital role in improving diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
