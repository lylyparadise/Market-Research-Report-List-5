<p><strong>The Submarine Power Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine power cable market primarily consists of two types: single-core cables and multi-core cables. Single-core cables are designed for high-voltage transmission, typically used in long-distance connections between offshore wind farms and the grid. They typically feature a single conductor, offering simplicity and efficiency. Multi-core cables, on the other hand, contain multiple conductors within a single sheath, allowing for more flexible installations and reduced space requirements. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable underwater power distribution and connectivity.</p></p>
