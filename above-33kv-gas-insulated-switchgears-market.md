<p><p>The Above 33KV Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) market is divided into two main types: Primary and Secondary Gas Insulated Switchgear. Primary GIS is designed for high-voltage applications, facilitating the transmission of electricity from substations to distribution points, ensuring a reliable and efficient power supply. Secondary GIS, on the other hand, is utilized for distribution purposes, typically found in urban areas, and helps in managing electricity at lower voltage levels. Both types enhance space efficiency and offer improved safety and maintenance benefits.</p></p>
