<p><p>The Overbed Tables market features three primary types: Pneumatic, Hydraulic, and Manual Overbed tables. Pneumatic Overbed tables use air pressure to adjust height effortlessly, providing users with convenience. Hydraulic Overbed tables rely on a hydraulic system for height adjustments, offering stability and ease of use. Manual Overbed tables require physical effort for height adjustments, typically using a hand crank or lever, making them cost-effective but less convenient. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences in healthcare and home settings.</p></p>
