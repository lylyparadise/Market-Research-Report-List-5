<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Veterinarian X-ray flat panel detector market is categorized into two types: direct conversion and indirect conversion. Direct conversion detectors utilize a semiconductor material to convert X-ray photons directly into electrical signals, offering high efficiency and improved image quality. In contrast, indirect conversion detectors first convert X-ray photons into visible light, which is then transformed into electronic signals using a photodetector. While direct conversion provides superior clarity, indirect conversion remains popular for its cost-effectiveness and established technology.</p></p>
