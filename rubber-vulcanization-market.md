<p><p>The rubber vulcanization market comprises several key types, including accelerators, vulcanizing agents, and activators. Accelerators enhance the speed and efficiency of the vulcanization process, making the rubber more durable. Vulcanizing agents, such as sulfur, chemically cross-link rubber molecules, providing essential strength and elasticity. Activators are substances that boost the effectiveness of vulcanizing agents, ensuring optimal performance during the cure process. Together, these components are critical in producing high-quality rubber products for various industrial applications.</p></p>
