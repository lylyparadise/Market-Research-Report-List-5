<p><strong>The Bio-Fertilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nitrogen-fixing Fertilizers</li><li>Phosphate-solubilizing Fertilizers</li><li>Potash-mobilizing Fertilizers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-Fertilizers are classified into different types based on their functions and nutrient content. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers help in fixing atmospheric nitrogen into a form that plants can absorb. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers convert insoluble phosphates into a soluble form for plant uptake. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers aid in releasing potassium from soil minerals for plant utilization. Other types of bio-fertilizers include micronutrient-rich fertilizers, organic matter-enriched fertilizers, and biopesticides. Each type of bio-fertilizer offers specific benefits for plant growth and soil health.</p></p>
