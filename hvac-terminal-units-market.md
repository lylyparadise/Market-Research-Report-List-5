<p><p>HVAC terminal units refer to devices that control the flow and temperature of air within a building. There are three main types of HVAC terminal units: Single Duct, Dual Duct, and Fan Powered. Single Duct units control both the supply and return air through a single duct system. Dual Duct units have separate ducts for hot and cold air, allowing for greater temperature control. Fan Powered units use a fan to propel air through the system, providing additional airflow and control options.</p></p>
