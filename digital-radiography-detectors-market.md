<p><strong>The Digital Radiography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Direct Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Computed radiography (CR) Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital radiography detectors market comprises three main types: Indirect Flat Panel Detectors, Direct Flat Panel Detectors, and Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors. Indirect Flat Panel Detectors utilize a two-step process where X-rays are first converted to light and then detected, offering high-quality images. Direct Flat Panel Detectors convert X-rays directly into electrical signals, providing faster imaging and reduced dose requirements. Computed Radiography uses photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, allowing for efficient image processing and digital storage. Each type serves distinct clinical needs and applications in medical imaging.</p></p>
