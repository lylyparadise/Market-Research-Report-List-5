<p><strong>The Industrial GigE Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial GigE Cameras market is primarily divided into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture complete images in a single shot, making them ideal for applications requiring high-resolution still images, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for inspecting continuous materials like textiles and conveyor belts. Both types utilize GigE technology for high-speed data transfer, enhancing automation efficiency.</p></p>
