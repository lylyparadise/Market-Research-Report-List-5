<p><p>In the semiconductor market, Quartz Glass Rods are primarily categorized into two types: Synthetic Quartz Rods and Fused Silica Rods. Synthetic Quartz Rods are produced through chemical vapor deposition, ensuring high purity and excellent thermal properties, making them ideal for precision applications. Fused Silica Rods, created by melting silicon dioxide, offer superior optical clarity and thermal stability. Both types are critical for manufacturing semiconductor devices, enabling efficient light transmission and exceptional durability in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
