<p><p>The greenhouse heating and cooling systems market comprises two main types: greenhouse heating systems and greenhouse cooling systems. Heating systems are designed to maintain optimal temperatures during colder seasons, utilizing options like radiant heaters, boilers, and electric systems. Conversely, greenhouse cooling systems help regulate temperature during warmer months, employing evaporative coolers, ventilation fans, and shade cloths. Both systems are essential for sustaining ideal growing conditions, maximizing plant growth, and enhancing crop yields throughout varying climates and seasons.</p></p>
