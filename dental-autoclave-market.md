<p><p>The dental autoclave market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automatic and automatic autoclaves. They offer basic features, allowing for user-friendly operation. In contrast, automatic autoclaves operate with minimal human input, utilizing advanced technology for precise sterilization cycles and monitoring.
