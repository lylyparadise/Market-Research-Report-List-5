<p><p>The micro syringe pump market is primarily categorized into 1-channel and 2-channel devices. A 1-channel micro syringe pump is designed to deliver fluid from a single syringe, ideal for applications requiring precise dosing from a single source. Conversely, a 2-channel micro syringe pump accommodates two syringes, allowing simultaneous delivery of different fluids or combinations, enhancing flexibility in experiments. This diversity enables customization in research and clinical settings, catering to varied requirements in drug delivery, biology, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
