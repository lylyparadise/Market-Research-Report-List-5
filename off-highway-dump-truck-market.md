<p><p>The Off-highway Dump Truck market includes two main types: Rigid Frame and Articulating Frame. Rigid Frame dump trucks are designed with a fixed chassis, offering high durability and stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty mining and construction tasks. Articulating Frame dump trucks feature a pivot joint between the cab and the bed, providing enhanced maneuverability and flexibility, suitable for uneven terrains and confined spaces. Both types cater to specific operational needs, balancing load capacity, terrain adaptability, and efficiency.</p></p>
