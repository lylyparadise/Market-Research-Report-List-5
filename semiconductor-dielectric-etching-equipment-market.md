<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Equipment</li><li>Dry Etching Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Dielectric Etching Equipment market comprises two primary types: wet etching equipment and dry etching equipment. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from semiconductor surfaces, offering high selectivity and uniformity, often employed for bulk material removal. In contrast, dry etching employs gases and plasma to create more precise patterns with better control over the etching process, making it essential for smaller feature sizes in advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Both methods are crucial for fabricating electronic components.</p></p>
