<p><p>The Flexo Plastic Printing Ink market comprises three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup, making them suitable for a variety of substrates. Solvent-based inks, known for their durability and adhesion, excel in outdoor applications despite higher VOC levels. UV-cured inks provide quick drying and vibrant colors through UV light exposure, making them ideal for high-speed production. Each type caters to different printing needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
