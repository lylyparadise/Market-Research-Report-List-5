<p><ul><li>Plunger High-Pressure Pump</li><li>Port and Helix High-Pressure Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive high-pressure pump market comprises various types, including plunger high-pressure pumps and port and helix high-pressure pumps. Plunger high-pressure pumps utilize a reciprocating mechanism to generate high pressure, making them ideal for fuel injection applications. In contrast, port and helix high-pressure pumps employ a helical rotor design that provides smooth flow and efficiency, suitable for hydraulic systems. Both types are essential for enhancing engine performance and ensuring efficient fuel delivery in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
