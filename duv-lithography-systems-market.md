<p><strong>The DUV Lithography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Dry Excimer Systems ­</li><li>ArF Immersion Excimer Systems ­</li><li>KrF Excimer Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DUV lithography systems market comprises several key types: ArF dry excimer systems utilize argon fluoride lasers for high-resolution patterning in semiconductor manufacturing, suitable for 200mm and 300mm wafers. ArF immersion excimer systems enhance resolution further by using a liquid medium between the lens and wafer, improving optics performance. KrF excimer systems, employing krypton fluoride lasers, are commonly used for less demanding applications, providing reliable patterns for advanced nodes, although they are gradually being supplemented by ArF technologies.</p></p>
