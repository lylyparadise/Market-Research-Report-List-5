<p><p>The gyrocopter engines market is primarily divided into two main types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines are lighter and simpler, offering high power-to-weight ratios, making them suitable for ultralight gyrocopters, though they may be less fuel-efficient and more polluting. In contrast, 4-stroke engines provide better fuel efficiency, reduced emissions, and greater longevity, making them favored for larger, more complex gyrocopters that require reliability and lower operational costs. The choice between them influences performance and operational needs.</p></p>
