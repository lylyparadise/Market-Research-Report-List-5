<p><p>The studio headphones and headsets market is categorized into three main types: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed-back headphones isolate sound, preventing external noise from interfering during recordings or mixing, making them ideal for studio environments. Semi-open back models offer a balance, providing some sound isolation while allowing a degree of sound leakage, enhancing the listening experience. Fully-open back headphones deliver an immersive audio experience by creating a natural soundstage, making them suitable for critical listening and monitoring.</p></p>
