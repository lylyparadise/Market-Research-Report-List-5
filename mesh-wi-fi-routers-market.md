<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers come in different market types, including Bi-Band, Tri-Band, and Quad-Band options. Bi-Band routers operate on two frequency bands, typically 2.4GHz and 5GHz, providing reliable connectivity for basic internet usage. Tri-Band routers add an additional 5GHz band, ideal for homes with multiple devices streaming HD content simultaneously. Quad-Band routers offer an even higher level of performance with four separate bands, ensuring seamless connectivity for smart homes and businesses with high-demand networking needs.</p></p>
