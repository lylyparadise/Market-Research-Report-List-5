<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li><li>Medium Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li><li>High Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid epoxy resin market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity types. Low viscosity epoxy resins are fluid and are ideal for applications requiring deep penetration and superior wetting properties. Medium viscosity resins strike a balance between flow and stability, making them suitable for general-purpose use. High viscosity epoxy resins are thick and provide excellent structural integrity; they are typically used in demanding applications where strength and durability are essential, such as coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
