<p><p>The Automotive Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) enhances vehicle safety by monitoring tire pressure. There are two primary types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors inside each tire to provide real-time pressure readings, offering precise information. In contrast, Indirect TPMS utilizes existing wheel speed sensors to estimate tire pressure based on comparisons of wheel rotation and can indicate low pressure but is less accurate. Both systems aim to prevent tire-related accidents and improve fuel efficiency.</p></p>
