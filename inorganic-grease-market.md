<p><p>The inorganic grease market primarily includes types like bentonite grease and silicone grease. Bentonite grease is characterized by its thickening properties from bentonite clay, providing excellent stability and high-temperature performance, making it ideal for extreme conditions. Conversely, silicone grease utilizes silicone compounds, offering superior moisture resistance and electrical insulation, making it suitable for automotive and electronic applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, addressing specific lubrication needs with their unique chemical compositions and properties.</p></p>
