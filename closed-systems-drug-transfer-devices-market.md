<p><p>Closed Systems Drug Transfer Devices (CSTD) are essential for ensuring safe handling of hazardous drugs in healthcare settings. The market is categorized into membrane-to-membrane systems and needleless systems. Membrane-to-membrane systems use a sealed membrane barrier to prevent contamination during drug transfer, ensuring a sterile environment. Needleless systems eliminate the use of needles, reducing the risk of needle-stick injuries and creating a safer workflow. Both types enhance safety and minimize the exposure of healthcare workers to hazardous substances.</p></p>
