<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Anti-lock Braking Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-channel ABS</li><li>Multi-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-wheeler anti-lock braking systems (ABS) are categorized into single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel ABS primarily controls the front wheel's braking, making it cost-effective and suitable for entry-level motorcycles. In contrast, multi-channel ABS manages both front and rear wheels, ensuring enhanced stability and safety during braking, especially in adverse conditions. Multi-channel systems are typically found in higher-end models, offering superior performance and rider confidence, thereby catering to a broader range of consumer needs in the motorcycle market.</p></p>
