<p><p>The ABS alloy market consists of various types, primarily ABS/PC (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene/Polycarbonate) and ABS/PET (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene/Polyethylene Terephthalate). ABS/PC combines the toughness of ABS with the heat resistance and transparency of polycarbonate, making it suitable for applications like automotive components and electronic housings. ABS/PET offers enhanced durability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for consumer goods and packaging. Other formulations include blends with different polymers to achieve specific properties for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
