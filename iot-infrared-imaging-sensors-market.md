<p><strong>The IoT Infrared Imaging Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The IoT infrared imaging sensors market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: short wave infrared (SWIR), medium wave infrared (MWIR), and long wave infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors, operating between 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, are ideal for applications like sensing in low light and through fog. MWIR sensors, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used for thermal imaging and gas detection. LWIR sensors, spanning 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in thermal energy detection, making them suitable for various surveillance and monitoring applications.</p></p>
