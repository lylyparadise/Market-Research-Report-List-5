<p><ul><li>Transport Infant Incubator</li><li>Normal Infant Incubator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant incubator market comprises two main types: transport infant incubators and normal infant incubators. Transport infant incubators are designed for the safe and stable transportation of premature or critically ill newborns, featuring lightweight, portable designs and battery-powered functionality for mobility. In contrast, normal infant incubators are used primarily in hospitals for long-term care, providing a controlled environment with precise temperature and humidity to support the health and development of vulnerable infants in a stationary setting.</p></p>
