<p><p>The SBR Cement Additive market includes two primary types: SBR Latex and SBR Powder. SBR Latex is a liquid polymer emulsion that enhances concrete's flexibility, adhesion, and durability, commonly used in waterproofing and repair applications. SBR Powder, a dry form of the additive, offers similar benefits but is easier to store, transport, and mix, making it ideal for dry-mix mortars and pre-blended construction materials. Both types improve concrete performance, with latex suited for wet applications and powder for dry formulations.</p></p>
