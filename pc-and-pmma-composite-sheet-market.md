<p><p>The PC (polycarbonate) and PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate) composite sheet market is categorized into two main types: two-layer and three-layer sheets. Two-layer sheets consist of a polycarbonate layer bonded to a PMMA layer, offering a balance of impact resistance and clarity. In contrast, three-layer sheets add an additional core layer, enhancing thermal insulation and structural strength. Both types are utilized in applications such as construction, signage, and automotive, benefiting from their lightweight nature and durability.</p></p>
