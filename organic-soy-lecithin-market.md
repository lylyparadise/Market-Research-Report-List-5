<p><ul><li>Fluid Organic Lecithins</li><li>De-oiled Organic Lecithin Powders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic soy lecithin market is primarily categorized into two types: fluid organic lecithins and de-oiled organic lecithin powders. Fluid organic lecithins are liquid emulsifiers used in food formulations, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, enhancing texture and stability. De-oiled organic lecithin powders, on the other hand, are derived by removing oil content, resulting in a dry form ideal for baking, dietary supplements, and health foods, providing concentrated phospholipids while maintaining an organic certification for health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
