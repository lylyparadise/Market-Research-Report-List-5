<p><p>Chlorinated Polyolefins (CPOs) are modified polymers with enhanced properties like chemical resistance, adhesion, and flame retardancy. **CPE (Chlorinated Polyethylene)** is widely used in wire insulation, automotive parts, and hoses due to its flexibility and durability. **CR (Chloroprene Rubber)** is valued for its weather resistance, used in seals and adhesives. **CPP (Chlorinated Polypropylene)** excels in coatings and adhesives for improved surface bonding. **CPVC (Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride)** is ideal for piping and fittings, offering high-temperature resistance and corrosion protection. Each type serves distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
