<p><p>The plastic frame fire windows market comprises two main types: fixed and movable fire windows. Fixed fire windows are designed to provide a permanent barrier against flames and smoke, ensuring safety in buildings during a fire. They are typically used in areas where unchanging views or access are required. In contrast, movable fire windows can be opened or closed as needed, offering both ventilation and fire protection. This flexibility makes them suitable for environments where airflow while maintaining safety is essential.</p></p>
