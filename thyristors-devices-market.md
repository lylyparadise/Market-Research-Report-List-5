<p><p>The thyristor devices market comprises several types, including SCR (Silicon Controlled Rectifiers), GTO (Gate Turn-Off Thyristors), IGCT (Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristors), and GCT (Gate Commutated Thyristors). SCRs are widely used for controlling power in various applications. GTOs allow for voltage control via gate pulses, enhancing performance in high-power systems. IGCTs combine switching speed and high efficiency suitable for industrial applications. GCTs provide high frequency and low conduction loss benefits. Other devices include Triacs and DIACs, offering diverse functionalities in power electronics.</p></p>
