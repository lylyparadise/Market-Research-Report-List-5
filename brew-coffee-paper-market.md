<p><p>The Brew Coffee Paper market consists of two primary types: non-heat-sealable and heat-sealable papers. Non-heat-sealable papers are designed for coffee brewing applications without the need for heat activation, making them easy to use but less effective at creating airtight seals. In contrast, heat-sealable papers utilize thermal properties to form a strong seal, ensuring freshness and preventing leakage during the brewing process. These options cater to different consumer preferences and packaging requirements in the coffee industry.</p></p>
