<p><p>Aircraft seat belts market can be classified into different types based on the number of attachment points. Two-point seat belts have a buckle and two attachment points on either side of the seat. Three-point seat belts have an additional attachment point that rests over the passenger's lap. Four-point seat belts have two additional attachment points that secure the passenger's shoulders. Other types of seat belts may include five-point harnesses or inflatable seat belts, offering varying levels of safety and comfort for passengers.</p></p>
