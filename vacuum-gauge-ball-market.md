<p><p>The vacuum gauge ball market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. The Shultz gauge offers precise measurements in low-pressure environments. The B-A gauge is known for its reliability in moderate vacuum conditions. Nude ion gauges excel in ultra-high vacuum scenarios, providing accurate readings with minimal contamination. Pirani gauges utilize thermal conductivity principles for effective pressure measurement in moderate vacuums. Thermocouple gauges combine thermoelectric properties to measure pressure, ensuring versatility across various industrial applications. Each type contributes uniquely to vacuum measurement technology.</p></p>
