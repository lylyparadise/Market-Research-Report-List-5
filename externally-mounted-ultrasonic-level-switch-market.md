<p><p>Externally mounted ultrasonic level switches are categorized into two main types: integral and split types. Integral type switches are self-contained units that combine the sensor and electronics in one unit, simplifying installation and reducing space requirements. In contrast, split type switches have separate sensor and electronics components, allowing for greater flexibility in positioning and installation, especially in challenging environments. Both types use ultrasonic technology to measure fluid levels, offering reliable performance across various applications.</p></p>
