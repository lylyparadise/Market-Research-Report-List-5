<p><p>The UV-curable resin market is divided into two primary types: non-acrylates and acrylates. Non-acrylates, including oligoamines, offer enhanced flexibility, lower viscosity, and better adhesion, making them suitable for specialized applications like coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, acrylates are widely used for their fast curing properties, excellent hardness, and durability in applications such as inks, coatings, and adhesives. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing performance and efficiency in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
