<p><ul><li>Wet Mix Shotcrete-sprayed Concrete</li><li>Dry Mix Shotcrete-sprayed Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shotcrete-sprayed concrete market consists of two primary types: wet mix and dry mix. Wet mix shotcrete involves pre-mixing cement, water, and aggregates, which are then pumped to a nozzle where compressed air applies the mixture. This method allows for better control over working conditions and material consistency. In contrast, dry mix shotcrete involves mixing dry ingredients before being pumped to the nozzle, where water is added during application. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on project requirements and site conditions.</p></p>
