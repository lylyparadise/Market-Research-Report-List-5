<p><p>Semiconductor Grade Encapsulants market can be categorized into One Component and Two Component types. Two Component encapsulants require mixing of two different components before application. They offer better control over the curing process and are ideal for more complex applications that require specific curing conditions. Both types play a crucial role in protecting semiconductor devices from environmental factors and ensuring their long-term reliability.</p></p>
