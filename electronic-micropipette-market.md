<p><strong>The Electronic Micropipette Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic micropipette market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multi-channel micropipettes. Single-channel micropipettes are designed for precise volume transfers from one location, ideal for tasks requiring high accuracy in small sample sizes. In contrast, multi-channel micropipettes enable simultaneous transfer of multiple samples, enhancing throughput and efficiency, particularly in high-throughput applications like PCR and ELISA. Both types cater to various laboratory needs, with single-channel focused on precision and multi-channel emphasizing productivity.</p></p>
