<p><p>The Portable Desktop Currency Detector market comprises two main detection types: fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection utilizes ultraviolet light to identify counterfeit currency by revealing unique features like fluorescence markings present on genuine notes. In contrast, magnetic detection employs magnetic sensors to distinguish real currency by detecting specific magnetic properties embedded in banknotes. Both methods aim to enhance security and accuracy in currency verification, making them essential tools for businesses and financial institutions in combating counterfeiting.</p></p>
