<p><strong>The Cutting Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooling</li><li>Lubrication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting fluid market primarily encompasses two key types: cooling fluids and lubrication fluids. Cooling fluids, such as water-based emulsions, help dissipate heat generated during machining processes, reducing tool wear and enhancing precision. In contrast, lubrication fluids, often oil-based, minimize friction between the tool and workpiece, improving surface finish and extending tool life. Both types are crucial in industrial applications, ensuring efficient machining operations by improving performance, accuracy, and the longevity of equipment while optimizing production processes.</p></p>
