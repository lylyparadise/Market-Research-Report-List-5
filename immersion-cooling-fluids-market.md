<p><p>The immersion cooling fluids market is primarily divided into two types: hydrocarbon and fluorocarbon. Hydrocarbon fluids, derived from petroleum sources, offer good thermal conductivity and are cost-effective options for data centers and electronics cooling. Fluorocarbon fluids, synthesized chemicals with high dielectric properties, excel in thermal management and are suitable for high-performance computing environments. Each type is chosen based on specific cooling requirements, environmental considerations, and operational efficiency, influencing their adoption across various industries.</p></p>
