<p><p>The **Heat Sterilization Equipment Market** includes **Dry Heat Sterilization Equipment** and **Moist Heat Sterilization Equipment**. Dry heat sterilization uses high temperatures (160°C–190°C) to eliminate microorganisms, ideal for heat-resistant materials like glassware and metals. Moist heat sterilization, typically using steam (autoclaves), operates at lower temperatures (121°C–134°C) and is effective for heat-sensitive items like medical instruments and lab media. Both methods ensure sterility but differ in application, efficiency, and compatibility with materials, catering to diverse industrial and medical needs.</p></p>
