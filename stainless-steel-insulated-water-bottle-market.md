<p><p>The stainless steel insulated water bottle market is primarily categorized into two types: non-vacuum stainless steel thermos cups and vacuum stainless steel thermos cups. Non-vacuum stainless steel thermos cups offer basic thermal insulation, suitable for short-term temperature retention. In contrast, vacuum stainless steel thermos cups utilize double-wall construction with an airless space between layers, providing superior insulation and keeping beverages hot or cold for extended periods. This distinction influences consumer choices based on functionality and intended use.</p></p>
