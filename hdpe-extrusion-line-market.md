<p><p>The HDPE extrusion line market is divided into two main types: single layer and multilayer. Single layer extrusion lines produce a uniform structure, ideal for straightforward applications like packaging and containers, emphasizing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multilayer extrusion lines create products with multiple layers that enhance barrier properties, durability, and functionality, making them suitable for specialized applications such as food packaging and medical supplies. This segmentation caters to varying performance needs and market demands.</p></p>
