<p><p>The fatty amines market segments into primary, secondary, and tertiary types based on their chemical structure. Primary fatty amines consist of one alkyl group connected to the nitrogen atom, making them effective surfactants and corrosion inhibitors. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl groups, enhancing their emulsifying properties. Tertiary fatty amines feature three alkyl groups, offering versatility in formulations and applications. Each type serves specific industrial uses, including detergents, personal care products, and chemical processing, contributing to market diversity and demand.</p></p>
