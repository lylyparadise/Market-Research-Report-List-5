<p><p>The Styrene-based Thermoplastic Elastomer (TPE) market comprises several key types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) offers excellent elasticity and toughness, ideal for adhesives and sealants. SIS (Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene) provides enhanced processability and low-temperature flexibility. SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene) features superior weather resistance and UV stability, making it suitable for outdoor applications. SEPS (Styrene-Ethylene-Propylene-Styrene) combines the qualities of SEBS with enhanced mechanical performance. Compound types involve mixing these elastomers with other materials for tailored properties. Other markets include niche applications and innovative formulations.</p></p>
