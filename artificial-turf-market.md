<p><ul><li>PE Artificial Grass Turf</li><li>PP Artificial Grass Turf</li><li>Nylon Artificial Grass Turf</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial turf market is primarily segmented into three types: PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and Nylon artificial grass. PE turf is known for its realistic feel and durability, making it popular for residential and commercial landscapes. PP turf is more affordable and often used for decorative purposes but lacks the durability of PE. Nylon turf is the strongest and most resilient option, suitable for high-traffic areas like sports fields, offering excellent performance and longevity. Each type serves unique applications and user needs.</p></p>
