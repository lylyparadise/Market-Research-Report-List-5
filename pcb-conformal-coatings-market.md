<p><p>PCB conformal coatings are used to protect printed circuit boards from moisture, dust, chemicals, and temperature fluctuations. There are five main types of conformal coatings in the market: Silicone, Parylene, Acrylic, Urethane, and Epoxy. Silicone coatings are flexible and durable, Parylene coatings are ultra-thin and offer high dielectric strength, Acrylic coatings are easy to apply and remove, Urethane coatings provide excellent chemical resistance, and Epoxy coatings offer strong mechanical protection. Each type has its own unique characteristics and suitability for different applications.</p></p>
